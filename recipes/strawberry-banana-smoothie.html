Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Description

This recipe is for a quick strawberry banana smoothie - perfect for the summer for a nice refresher or after you go to the gym! This recipe is easy to make with 4 simple ingredients in less than 5 minutes. Definitely my go-to smoothie!

Ingredients

  • Frozen strawbeerries
  • Fresh bananas or frozen bananas
  • Milk (any type)
  • Ice (however much is needed for desired consistency)

Optional Variations

  • Vanilla: If you want extra flavor, you can add in a 1/2 teaspoon or so of vanilla extract.
  • Greek yogurt: You can also add 1/2 cup of plain (or vanilla) Greek yogurt to make the smoothie a little more creamy.
  • Powders: You can also add a scoop of protein, acai, or maca powder.
  • Greens: Toss in a large handful of fresh baby spinach or kale to make it a green smoothie (if you want to feel healthy)
  • Honey: I personally like to add a scoop or two of honey to add some flavor!

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth. If the smoothie seems to thick, add in a bit of extra milk or water. If it seems too thin, add in more strawbeerries or banana.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a serving glass or two and enjoy!
