Avocado Toast

Description

This recipe is an avocado toast, one of my favorite quick and easy to make breakfasts. This will contain a basic recipe with some tips and variations to try out. :D

Ingredients

Just toast and avocado! Different ingredients may be needed depending on the variation being made.

Tips

  • Mashed avocado is more creamy and luxurious than sliced avocado.
  • You'll want to mix in at least a pinch of salt per avocado half.
  • Add garlic: Lightly rub a peeled raw garlic clove over the top of your toast before adding the avocado, or mix a tiny pinch of garlic powder into the avocado.
  • Put an egg on it!
  • Try with some hot sauce!
