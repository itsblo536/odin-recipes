Breakfast Quesadilla

Description

This recipe is for a simple bacon, egg, and cheese quesadilla. This recipe takes 5 mins to prep and 10 mins to cook and is sufficient for 2 servings of quesadillas.There are 476 calories per serving.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 teaspoon butter, softened, or to taste
  • Cooking spray
  • 1/2 cup shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a small bowl. Add crumbled bacon and whisk until well combined.
  2. Spray a large skillet with cooking spray; warm over medium - high heat. Pour egg mixture into the skillet; cook and stir until eggs are set, about 5 minutes. Remmove from the heat.
  3. Lightly butter one side of each tortilla.
  4. Warm a separate, dry skillet over medium - low heat.
  5. Place one tortilla into the warm skilelt with the buttered side facing down. Spoon 1/2 of the scrambled egg mixture over one half of the tortilla. Sprinkle 1/4 cup Colby-Jack cheese over the egg mixture and fold the tortilla over top. Cook, turning as needed, until tortilla is toasted and crispy and cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat to make second quesadilla.
